# Benchmarks

Generated by `python bench/run.py`. Sources are pinned sdists from PyPI, so these numbers are reproducible.


## Cost of staying current

| package | files | functions | cold | warm | 1-file edit | dependents dirtied |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| requests 2.32.3 | 18 | 240 | 80 ms | 3 ms | 8 ms | 5 |
| flask 3.0.3 | 24 | 353 | 100 ms | 1 ms | 6 ms | 21 |
| celery 5.4.0 | 158 | 2888 | 620 ms | 4 ms | 9 ms | 6 |
| sqlalchemy 2.0.36 | 255 | 10413 | 2623 ms | 3 ms | 13 ms | 39 |
| django 5.2.16 | 883 | 9213 | 2608 ms | 50 ms | 67 ms | 2 |

A stateless analyzer pays the *cold* column on every invocation. An agent making 40 edits pays it 40 times.


## Blast radius

Files implicated by a change to one file, computed for every file in the package.

| package | median | p75 | p90 | p99 | max | mean | <=10 files | largest import cycle |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| requests 2.32.3 | 6 | 8 | 9 | 14 | 14 | 6 | 94.4% | 1 (6%) |
| flask 3.0.3 | 22 | 22 | 22 | 23 | 23 | 19.5 | 12.5% | 19 (79%) |
| celery 5.4.0 | 6 | 78 | 89 | 114 | 123 | 41.2 | 51.3% | 34 (22%) |
| sqlalchemy 2.0.36 | 40 | 245 | 245 | 246 | 248 | 127.1 | 27.1% | 121 (47%) |
| django 5.2.16 | 3 | 68 | 571 | 573 | 588 | 140.4 | 58.8% | 162 (18%) |

Median far below mean is the signal: most changes are cheap to verify, a minority are not. The expensive minority are the files inside the largest import cycle.
